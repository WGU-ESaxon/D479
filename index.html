Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to my hotel from the airport?

Taxis and hotel shuttles available. Bus service also connects airport to town.

Can I drink the tap water?

Yes, tap water is safe to drink throughout the island.

What's the weather like year-round?

Tropical climate, 75-85¬∞F year-round. Rainy season June-November.

What's the best time to visit?

Year-round tropical climate, dry season from May to October.

Do I need a visa?

Most visitors can stay up to 30 days without a visa.

What currency is used?

US Dollar is widely accepted alongside local currency.
